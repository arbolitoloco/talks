NEON

The National Ecological Observatory Network (NEON) is a large facility project funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF). NEON is a continental-scale platform for ecological research. The observatory will gather long-term data to enable fundamental research on biological responses to shifting environmental conditions, land-use changes, and invasive species.
