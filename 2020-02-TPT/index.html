NEON

The National Ecological Observatory Network (NEON) is a large facility project funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF). NEON is a continental-scale platform for ecological research. The observatory will gather long-term data to enable fundamental research on biological responses to shifting environmental conditions, land-use changes, and invasive species.

associatedTaxa in omoccurrences table: verbatim scinames (list), host:, etc. this can be edited in: https://serv.biokic.asu.edu/sandbox/collections/editor/occurrenceeditor.php; 'Associated Taxa'. Record-based.
Trait Module: Phenology example. Trait mining with the Occurrence Trait Coding Tools Occurrence Association Manager (https://serv.biokic.asu.edu/sandbox/collections/misc/assocmanagement.php?collid=1) will mine traits from fields in occurrences and add them to the table omoccurrassociations.
Table omoccurrassociations has: hard association (specimen/specimen in same system), relationship (define type of relationship), association with specimen in another db (resourceurl), verbatimsciname, verify names by indexing in internal thesaurus, verify through external source, dynamicProperties (could hold all that information if they'd like).
